Visit Cork

Enjoy history, wildlife, and nightlife in this vibrant county in the south of Ireland.

Top three activities to do in Cork

Horseriding

Cork has many horseriding locations where you can explore true rural life and enjoy the scenery

Grab a pint of local stout

Cork City is packed with old pubs and bars where you can taste the local stouts in true Irish fashion

Find a beach

Despite its cold, Cork has many beaches where you'll find patrons swimming in the mornings to freshen up
